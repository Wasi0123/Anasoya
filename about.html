Our Story

Welcome to Grillardes Anna Soya

Founded in the heart of Montreal in 2020, Grillardes Anna Soya was born from a passion for authentic African flavors and a desire to share the rich culinary heritage of Cameroon with the vibrant city of Montreal. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the finest Cameroonian and African grilling experiences to your table.

What began as a weekend hobby soon grew into a thriving business as word spread about our unique flavors and commitment to quality. Today, we're proud to serve both individual customers and event organizers across Montreal, bringing the authentic taste of perfectly grilled meats to every occasion.

At Grillardes Anna Soya, we take pride in our Cameroonian roots. Our recipes are inspired by generations of culinary tradition, passed down through families and perfected over time. From our signature soya (suya), every grill tells a story of African heritage and passion for flavor.
