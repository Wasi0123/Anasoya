Our Story

Welcome to Grillardes Anna Soya

Founded in the heart of Montreal, Grillardes Anna Soya began as a small family operation with a big passion for authentic grilling. Our journey started at local farmers' markets, where we introduced Montrealers to our signature soya - tender, flavorful roasted beef that quickly became a customer favorite.

What began as a weekend hobby soon grew into a thriving business as word spread about our unique flavors and commitment to quality. Today, we're proud to serve both individual customers and event organizers across Montreal, bringing the authentic taste of perfectly grilled meats to every occasion.

Our name "Anna Soya" pays homage to the family matriarch whose recipes and cooking techniques form the foundation of our offerings. Her traditional approach to marinating and slow-roasting meats has been passed down through generations, and we're proud to continue her legacy.
