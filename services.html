Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for smaller events and 4-6 weeks for larger events or during peak season (May-September).

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Montreal and surrounding areas. Additional travel fees may apply for events outside our standard service area.

Do you provide utensils, plates, and serving equipment?

Our packages include grilling equipment and serving tools for the food we prepare. We can provide disposable plates and utensils for an additional fee, or you may choose to provide your own.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions with advance notice. Please include any special requirements in your booking form.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. No refunds for cancellations within 7 days of the event.
