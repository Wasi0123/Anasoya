Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products

What is the shelf life of your spices?

Our spices have a shelf life of 12 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery throughout Montreal and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on distance.

Can I customize my spice blend?

Absolutely! We offer custom spice blends tailored to your specific tastes. Contact us to discuss your preferences.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting us directly via phone, email, or through our contact form. We'll guide you through the ordering process.
