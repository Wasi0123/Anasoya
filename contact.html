Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ordering and our services

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location in Montreal. Generally, orders placed before 2 PM will be delivered the same day. Orders after 2 PM will be delivered the next day. You'll receive a confirmation message with an estimated delivery time after placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, Interac e-Transfer, and PayPal. For larger orders or event services, we require a 25% deposit to secure your booking.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order up to 2 hours before your scheduled delivery or pickup time. Please call us directly at (514) 123-4567 for any changes to your order.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions including gluten-free, dairy-free, and certain allergies. Please specify your requirements in the "Special Instructions" section when placing your order.

How far in advance should I book for events?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for smaller events and 4-6 weeks for larger events like weddings. However, we understand that sometimes events come together quickly, so please contact us even for last-minute requests.
